### On-Page SEO Requirements

**Title Tags (per page):**
- Home: "Alcoa Visuals | Video Production in Knoxville, Tennessee"
- Video Services: "Corporate Video Production | Knoxville TN | Alcoa Visuals"
- Photography: "Corporate Photography Services | Knoxville | Alcoa Visuals"

- About: "About Brad Coulter | Alcoa Visuals | Knoxville Videographer"

**Meta Descriptions:** Write unique 150-160 character descriptions for each page including location and primary service keywords.

**Header Structure:** Each page should have one H1 containing primary keyword, with H2s for major sections.

**Image Alt Text:** Descriptive alt text on all images including location keywords where natural.

**Internal Linking:** Cross-link between related services (video ↔ photography, services → contact).

### Local SEO

- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all pages
- Embed Google Map on contact page
- Include location references naturally in content (Alcoa, Knoxville, East Tennessee, Blount County)

## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Strategy

### Goal
Position Alcoa Visuals content to appear in AI-generated answers and featured snippets.

### FAQ Content

Add FAQ sections to key pages answering common questions:

*Video Services page:*
- "How much does corporate video production cost in Knoxville?"
- "What's included in a video production package?"
- "How long does it take to produce a corporate video?"

*Photography Services page:*
- "What is corporate photography used for?"
- "Do you do product photography for e-commerce?"

*Workshops page:*
- "What will I learn in a photography workshop?"
- "Do I need my own camera for photography classes?"

### Structured Data

- LocalBusiness schema (all pages)
- Service schema (service pages)
- FAQPage schema (FAQ sections)

### Content Approach

- Write clear, direct answers in the first sentence of sections
- Use question-based H2s where appropriate
- Include "People Also Ask" style content naturally

### Authority Signals

- Mention Brad's UT Creative Writing degree
- Reference years of experience
- Include client types served (broadcast TV, corporate, nonprofit)
